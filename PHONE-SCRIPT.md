# 📱 Scripture Studio — Phone Video Script (~2.5 min)

Talk like you're showing a friend. Short sentences. Be yourself.
Tip: film your FACE for the intro + outro, screen-record the DEMO for the middle.

---

## 🎬 INTRO — (your face, or just start talking)

"What's up — I'm Denzel. I'm 22, and I built a Bible app called **MyBibleLens**. It's live on the App Store — I made the whole thing myself.

MyBibleLens is about experiencing Scripture not just as words to read… but as a **living landscape** to explore, create, and share.

So for this challenge I asked one question: what if the Word could show up *right inside* the moment you're already creating in? Let me show you."

---

## 📲 THE DEMO — (screen-record the demo now)

*(open scripture-studio.pages.dev)*

"This is **Scripture Studio** — my Infinite Canvas. A living landscape. See this note? Youth night, the theme is *'who God says you are'* — I need a verse.

So I just… talk to it."

*(tap ✦ Compose → tap "Sermon slide")*

*(verse lands on the canvas)*

"And watch — **Gloo's AI** reads the moment and picks the verse. First Peter 2:9 — *'you are a chosen people, a royal priesthood.'* **YouVersion** gives me the real text. And it lands right here, on my canvas, next to my note.

**Scripture didn't make me leave what I was doing. It showed up inside it.**"

*(drag the card around a little — show it's alive)*

*(tap ＋ Sermon Deck → tap the "Sermon Deck" tab)*

"One tap — and that verse becomes a slide in my sermon deck. I could build my whole message this way."

*(tap the "Reflect" tab)*

"And every moment I create gets saved as a reflection — so I can always come back to it."

---

## 🙏 CLOSE — (your face again, or voiceover)

"This isn't another Bible app. It's Scripture showing up where people already are.

I already shipped MyBibleLens — by myself — before I ever found this challenge. This is the frontier it's growing into.

Billions of moments of creation happen every day. I want the Word to be in them. That's the bridge. Thank you."

---

**The one line to nail:** *"Scripture didn't make me leave what I was doing. It showed up inside it."*
**Keep it under 3:00. Do a practice run first so the demo loads smooth. You got this. 🎬**
