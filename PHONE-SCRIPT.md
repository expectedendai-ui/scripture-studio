# 📱 Scripture Studio — Phone Video Script (~2.5 min)

Talk like you're showing a friend. Short sentences. Be yourself.
Tip: film your FACE for the intro + outro, screen-record the DEMO for the middle.

---

## 🎬 INTRO — (your face, or just start talking)

"What's up — I'm Denzel. I'm 22, and I built a Bible app called **MyBibleLens**, and it's living live in the App Store right now!

MyBibleLens is about experiencing Scripture not just as words to read… but as a **living landscape** to explore, create, and share.

So for this challenge I asked one question: what if the Word could show up *right inside* the moment you're already creating in? Let me show you."

---

## 📲 THE DEMO — (screen-record scripture-studio.pages.dev)

**(1) Show the living canvas**
"This is my Infinite Canvas. Look — it's already alive. My tools are right here on the toolbar, and my Scripture is connected to real photos and moments — Sunday service, small group, camp — all tied together with these gold threads. This is what my app does: it weaves the Word into your actual life."

**(2) Compose a verse**
"See this note? Youth night — *'who God says you are'* — I need a verse. So I just… talk to it." *(tap ✦ Compose → tap "Sermon slide")*

**(3) The money moment** *(verse lands on the canvas)*
"Watch — **Gloo's AI** reads the moment and picks the verse. First Peter 2:9. **YouVersion** gives me the real text. And it lands right here, on my canvas, next to my note.
**Scripture didn't make me leave what I was doing. It showed up inside it.**" *(drag the card a little — show it's alive)*

**(4) It flows into a sermon** *(tap ＋ Sermon Deck → tap the "Sermon Deck" tab)*
"One tap and that verse joins my sermon deck — here's a whole message, *'Who God says you are,'* slide by slide. I built this just by talking."

**(5) It remembers** *(tap the "Reflect" tab)*
"And every moment I create is saved as a reflection — the intent, the verse, what it spoke. My whole history with the Word, kept."

---

## 🙏 CLOSE — (your face again)

"This isn't another Bible app. It's Scripture showing up where people already are.

I asked this question years ago… then I built the answer — by myself, before I ever found this challenge. This is the frontier it's growing into.

Billions of moments of creation happen every day. I want the Word to be in them. That's the bridge. Thank you."

---

**The one line to nail:** *"Scripture didn't make me leave what I was doing. It showed up inside it."*
**Keep it under 3:00. Practice-run once so the demo loads smooth. You got this. 🎬**
