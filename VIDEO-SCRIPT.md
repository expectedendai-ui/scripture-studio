# Scripture Studio — 3-Minute Video Script

**Goal:** win the "Scripture in New Frontiers" challenge (Gloo × YouVersion).
**Judging:** Impact & Vision 40 · Video Storytelling 30 · Technical 30. The video is the primary lens — tell a STORY, show it WORKING.
**Format:** ≤3:00, public on YouTube. Screen-record the demo (scripture-studio.pages.dev) + your voice over it. A talking-head open/close is optional but powerful.
**Voice:** you — young founder, faith-driven, real. Don't perform. Talk like you'd tell a friend.

---

## SCENE 1 — THE HOOK (0:00–0:25)
**On screen:** You, to camera (or a black screen with the words as you say them).
**You say:**
> "Billions of people spend their whole lives creating in digital spaces — posts, stories, slides, art. And in all of that creating… Scripture almost never shows up. Not because it doesn't belong there. Because nobody built the bridge. So I built one."

*(Beat. Then cut to the demo.)*

---

## SCENE 2 — WHO YOU ARE (0:25–0:45)
**On screen:** quick flash of MyBibleLens on the App Store / your phone, then the Scripture Studio canvas.
**You say:**
> "I'm Denzel. I'm 22, still in college, and I already ship a Bible app — MyBibleLens, live on the App Store. My whole app is built on one idea: Scripture isn't just words to read, it's a living landscape to explore and create in. So for this challenge I asked — what if the Word could show up *inside* the moment you're already making?"

---

## SCENE 3 — THE BRIDGE (0:45–2:10) — the heart of the video
**On screen:** screen recording of the demo. Move slowly, let each beat land.

**(a) The living canvas (0:45)**
> "This is Scripture Studio. It's my Infinite Canvas — a living landscape. Here's a note I left myself: youth night, theme is *who God says you are*, need a verse."

**(b) Compose (1:00)** — tap ✦ Compose, type or speak the moment.
> "So I just… talk to it. 'A sermon slide on identity for youth night — who God says you are.'"

**(c) The money shot (1:15)** — the verse card lands on the canvas.
> "And watch — Gloo's faith-tuned AI reads the moment and chooses the verse. 1 Peter 2:9 — *you are a chosen people, a royal priesthood.* YouVersion delivers the real, licensed text. And it lands right here, on my canvas, next to my note. Scripture didn't make me leave what I was doing. It showed up *inside* it." *(drag the card a little — show it's alive)*

**(d) Sermon Deck (1:35)** — tap ＋ Sermon Deck, then the Sermon Deck tab.
> "One tap, and that verse becomes a slide in my sermon deck. I could build the whole message this way — speak a moment, get the Word, build the talk."

**(e) Reflect (1:50)** — tap the Reflect tab.
> "And every moment I compose is saved as a reflection — what I was making, the verse, what it spoke. A quiet record to come back to."

**(f) Language (2:00)** — switch dropdown to Español, compose one line.
> "And it's not just English. Un versículo para mi hermano — and it answers, and quotes Scripture, in Spanish. This works for the whole Church."

---

## SCENE 4 — IT'S REAL (2:10–2:35)
**On screen:** flash the GitHub repo / the two API names.
**You say:**
> "Both APIs, actually working — not a mockup. Gloo AI Studio does the reasoning, the YouVersion Platform delivers the Scripture, and the code is open source. This is real, and it runs live right now."

---

## SCENE 5 — THE VISION / CLOSE (2:35–3:00)
**On screen:** back to you, or the canvas with a few verses on it.
**You say:**
> "This isn't another Bible app. It's Scripture showing up where people already create. MyBibleLens is live on the App Store today — and this is the frontier it's growing into. Billions of moments of creation happen every day. I want the Word to be in them. That's the bridge. Thanks for watching."

*(End card: "Scripture Studio · built on YouVersion Platform + Gloo AI Studio · scripture-studio.pages.dev")*

---

## SHOT LIST / TIPS
- **Record the screen at 1080p**, browser full-screen, on the live demo. Do a dry run first so the compose responses come back smooth.
- **Pre-load the canvas** with the sticky note (it's already there) so the "answer my note" beat works.
- If a compose is slow on camera, that's fine — cut the dead air in editing, or say "give it a second — it's really thinking."
- **Talk-head bookends** (Scene 1 + 5 on camera) make judges connect with the founder. The middle is screen + voiceover.
- Keep it UNDER 3:00. If tight, trim Scene 2 — the demo is what wins.
- Your existing video toolchain (ffmpeg, etc.) can stitch screen-record + voiceover + captions. Happy to help cut it.

## THE ONE LINE THAT WINS
> "Scripture didn't make me leave what I was doing. It showed up inside it."
Say it clearly on the money shot. That sentence *is* the whole challenge.
